Code of Conduct

kabootr is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone. By participating in this project, you agree to be respectful to others, to avoid abusive language, and to engage in constructive, professional dialogue.

If you encounter or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers at: contact@example.com

Our expectations include:

- Be courteous and professional in all interactions
- Do not harass or discriminate against others based on race, gender, sexuality, disability, age, or any other characteristic
- Be mindful of how your words and actions affect others
- Report concerns privately to the project maintainers

We will review reports and take appropriate action, which may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from the project.
